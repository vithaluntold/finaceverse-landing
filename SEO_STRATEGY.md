# FinACEverse SEO Strategy & Google Visibility Guide

## Completed SEO Optimizations

### 1. Technical SEO ✅
- **robots.txt**: Created with proper directives for search engines
- **sitemap.xml**: XML sitemap with all pages, priorities, and update frequencies
- **Canonical URLs**: Added to all pages to prevent duplicate content
- **Structured Data**: JSON-LD schema markup for Organization and SoftwareApplication

### 2. On-Page SEO ✅
- **Meta Titles**: Optimized with keywords and under 60 characters
- **Meta Descriptions**: Compelling descriptions with CTAs, under 160 characters
- **Keywords**: Strategic placement of target keywords
- **Open Graph Tags**: Complete OG tags for social sharing
- **Twitter Cards**: Enhanced Twitter sharing appearance
- **Mobile Optimization**: Responsive design with proper viewport

### 3. Content Optimization ✅
- **H1 Tags**: Single, keyword-rich H1 on each page
- **Semantic HTML**: Proper heading hierarchy (H1-H6)
- **Alt Tags**: Images should have descriptive alt text
- **Internal Linking**: Strategic cross-linking between pages
